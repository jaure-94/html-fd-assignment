My Favourite Books

I have 2 main kinds of books I like to read:

  1. Software engineering books
  2. Self-help, non-fiction books

Software engineering Books

One of my reading habits is centered around gaining as much knowledge as I can about IT and software engineering.

Here are my favourites:

  • Eloquent JavaScript - Marijn Haverbeke
  • Cracking the Coding Interview
  • Software Engineering - Ian Sommerville
  • Don't Make Me Think - Steve Krug
  • PHP and MySQL - W. Jason Gilmore

Self-Help, Non-Fiction Books

These books help me to organize my mind, which in turn helps me to organize my life! I recommend these to anyone seeking to improve their lives.

Here are my favourites:

  • Atomic Habits - James Clear
  • The 5AM Club - Robin Sharma
  • Grit - Angela Duckworth
  • Deep Work - Cal Newport
  • Zero To One - Peter Thiel
